# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

**Seekers** — people in Belgium (and neighbouring Dutch-speaking regions) looking to start or deepen a wellness career: massage therapists, nail technicians, reflexologists, yoga teachers, nutritionists. Often balancing a day job with evening or weekend study. They browse on mobile, filter by location and lesson time, and need transparent info before committing.

**Providers** — verified wellness academies and independent trainers who want qualified leads. They manage their listing, courses, and incoming inquiries through the dashboard.

## Product Purpose

Blissify is a curated discovery platform for wellness training in Belgium. It exists because the market is fragmented, unverified, and opaque — prices are hidden, accreditation unclear, and good schools are invisible next to SEO farms. Blissify's promise: every listed provider is hand-verified; every course shows price, duration, accreditation, and schedule up front.

Success means a seeker finds and contacts the right course in under 5 minutes; a provider gets qualified inquiries without needing their own marketing.

## Brand Personality

Gezaghebbend · Warm · Professioneel (Authoritative, Warm, Professional)

The brand speaks with quiet editorial authority — like a respected guide rather than a sales pitch. It is warm without being soft, professional without being clinical.

## Anti-references

- **Generic SaaS startup templates** — gradient-accent CTAs, feature-bullet grids, "everything has rounded-pill borders" aesthetic.
- **Spa/wellness clichés** — lotus flowers, soft pastels, floating bubbles, lavender gradients, chakra iconography.
- **AI-generated landing pages** — cream body background, tiny tracked uppercase eyebrows on every section, hero-metric template, identical icon-card grids.
- **Overly playful/bubbly UI** — pill-on-everything, soft drop shadows, bouncy micro-animations, baby-shower color palette.

## Design Principles

1. **Authority through restraint** — trust is earned with substance (verified badges, real stats, transparent pricing) not with decoration. When in doubt, remove rather than add.
2. **Warmth without cliché** — warmth lives in copy tone, generous whitespace, and human photography. Never in lotus imagery or pastel softness.
3. **Curated, not crowded** — the platform's core value is that it selects for quality. The UI should feel like a well-edited magazine, not a marketplace with everything fighting for attention.
4. **Transparent by default** — price, duration, accreditation, and location are always visible. No hidden details, no "contact us for pricing."
5. **Consistent craft at every depth** — the dashboard and provider pages should feel as considered as the homepage. Quality doesn't stop at login.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA as minimum. Body text must meet 4.5:1 against its background. Respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Platform is Dutch-language first (`lang="nl"`), with future FR/DE expansion planned. No motion that cannot be turned off.
